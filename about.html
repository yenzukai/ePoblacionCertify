Community Profile

San Vicente, formerly known as Poblacion, is a picturesque and culturally rich barangay nestled within the charming municipality of Lagonoy, situated in the heart of the captivating province of Camarines Sur, Philippines. This vibrant community, which has a history as colorful as the landscape surrounding it, boasts a unique identity that is deeply woven into the tapestry of Camarines Sur's heritage.

As of the most recent data available from the 2020 Census, San Vicente had a population of 1,157 residents, making it a pivotal component of Lagonoy's demographic landscape. This number represents approximately 2.04% of the total population of the municipality, underlining the significance of San Vicente in contributing to the overall social fabric of Lagonoy.

San Vicente's residents, who are a diverse mix of individuals from various backgrounds and walks of life, coexist harmoniously and share a strong sense of community spirit. They take pride in their rich cultural traditions, which are showcased through vibrant festivals, local events, and a deep-rooted appreciation for their unique history.

The barangay's scenic beauty is further enhanced by its natural surroundings. The lush greenery, serene rivers, and the gentle rustling of leaves in the cool breeze create an idyllic atmosphere that residents and visitors alike find enchanting. San Vicente is a place where one can escape the hustle and bustle of urban life and find solace in the simplicity of rural living.

In addition to its cultural and natural attractions, San Vicente plays a vital role in the economic life of Lagonoy. The barangay is home to various small businesses, shops, and local enterprises that cater to the needs of both its residents and tourists who seek to explore the beauty of Camarines Sur. Agriculture also thrives in this community, contributing to the region's agricultural productivity and sustainability.
